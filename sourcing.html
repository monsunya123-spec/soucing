How Sourcing Works

A transparent, step-by-step process from inquiry to delivery

1

Requirement Analysis

We discuss your product needs, budget, quality expectations, and timeline to create a detailed sourcing brief.

2

Supplier Research

Our team searches Alibaba, trade shows, and factory databases to find 3-5 qualified suppliers that match your requirements.

3

Verification & Sampling

We verify supplier credentials, negotiate pricing, and coordinate sample production and evaluation.

4

Production & QC

Once samples are approved, we oversee production, conduct quality inspections, and provide progress reports.

5

Logistics & Delivery

We arrange freight, consolidation, customs clearance, and final delivery to your door.
