How Sourcing Works

A transparent, step-by-step process from inquiry to delivery

1

Requirement Analysis

Before Buyzo

You know what you want, but turning ideas into clear specifications is harder than it sounds. Designs keep changing. Feedback loops drag on. Samples miss expectations. Timelines slip. Most production problems don't start in the factory — they start with unclear requirements.

With Buyzo

We turn concepts into production-ready specifications. Ambiguity becomes clarity. Everyone works from the same playbook. When requirements are right from the beginning, everything else becomes easier.

2

Supplier Research

Before Buyzo

Finding the right supplier takes time. Quotes are difficult to compare. Capabilities are unclear. Responses vary. And it's often impossible to know whether you're dealing directly with a factory or another layer in between.

With Buyzo

We evaluate hundreds of manufacturers and shortlist only the few that truly fit your needs. Verified capabilities, proven production experience, and transparent comparisons — so you can make decisions with confidence, not guesswork.

3

Verification & Sampling

Before Buyzo

The sample looks perfect. Production begins. Then the finished goods arrive, and something feels different. Colors shift. Dimensions vary. Materials aren't quite the same. Small inconsistencies become expensive surprises.

With Buyzo

We establish a Golden Sample, define acceptance standards, and monitor consistency throughout production. What gets approved is what gets delivered — with no surprises along the way.

4

Production & Quality Control

Before Buyzo

Once production starts, visibility disappears. Updates are limited. Problems surface late. By the time issues are discovered, options are few and costs are high.

With Buyzo

We provide visibility throughout the entire production process. Standards are set upfront. Inspections happen during production, not after. Reports, photos, and feedback keep you informed every step of the way, wherever you are.

5

Logistics & Delivery

Before Buyzo

Shipping shouldn't feel complicated, yet every stage brings another question. Freight options, customs paperwork, destination clearance, unexpected charges — managing international logistics often means coordinating countless moving pieces.

With Buyzo

From factory pickup to final delivery, we manage the entire journey. Shipping, customs, documentation, and last-mile delivery are all handled for you. Simply tell us where it needs to go and when you need it there. We'll take care of the rest.
