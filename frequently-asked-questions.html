Frequently Asked Questions

Explore some of our most commonly asked questions below. If you don't find what you are looking for, you are welcome to contact us with your specific requirements.

Sourcing and Supply

How do you charge? Are there any hidden fees?

Buy Direct

There is no setup fee. You simply pay the product cost plus a 10% commission.

Our service includes supplier verification, sample coordination, contract negotiation, quality control, logistics management, and after-sales support.

No hidden fees. Everything is clearly communicated before we start.

Private Label & ODM

We recommend starting with a free 20-minute consultation so we can understand your project.

Based on your requirements, we evaluate project complexity, product development requirements, order volume, and timeline.

We then provide a customized proposal, which may include a one-time setup fee depending on the project and a commission rate, typically between 10% and 15%, depending on order value.

All costs are confirmed in writing before any work begins. No unexpected charges or price increases.

How do you select suppliers?

Our Expertise

We've worked within China's supply chain ecosystem for more than 20 years, with deep experience in furniture, home decor, e-bikes, automotive parts, food additives, and pet products.

How We Vet Factories

We don't simply maintain a supplier database. We build long-term relationships and evaluate factories through major domestic and international trade shows, regular factory visits, and deep knowledge of China's manufacturing clusters.

For example: furniture in Foshan, electronics in Shenzhen, and hardware products in Yongkang.

Every factory we recommend has been carefully observed and validated over time.

What if I'm not satisfied with the products?

Prevention Comes First

Quality control happens at three stages: before production, during production, and after production.

Before production, we define standards. During production, we monitor progress and perform inspections. After production, we conduct final quality inspection.

If Issues Still Occur

We help identify the root cause, communicate with the factory on your behalf, coordinate solutions such as rework, replacement, or partial refunds, and follow through until the issue is resolved.

We don't disappear after shipment. We stay on your side and work toward a fair solution.

How do you ensure quality?

We rely on three layers of quality control.

Before Production

We confirm product specifications, seal and preserve an approved Golden Sample, and define inspection standards.

During Production

We monitor production progress, conduct in-process inspections, and identify issues before they become costly problems.

After Production

We perform final inspections based on AQL standards and approve shipment only after products meet requirements.

We don't rely solely on the factory's own quality system. We use independent quality control processes to protect your interests.

Will I receive regular updates?

Absolutely. Depending on the stage of your project, we provide regular updates.

During requirement analysis, updates are provided as needed for requirement confirmation and solution development. During supplier selection, sampling, and production, updates are typically weekly. During shipping, we provide real-time updates for departure, arrival, customs clearance, and estimated delivery dates.

We also provide English and Chinese reports, photos and videos when needed, and scheduled voice or video calls. You'll always know where things stand.

How quickly do you respond?

Typical response times are within 2 hours on WeChat or WhatsApp during business days, within 24 hours by email, and by appointment for phone or video calls.

We understand time zone differences and do our best to respond promptly. For urgent matters, we're always reachable.

Can I communicate directly with the factory?

Yes. We support two approaches.

Option A — Through Buyzo (Recommended)

We act as your bridge by filtering information, handling translations, reducing misunderstandings, and improving communication efficiency. You don't need to deal with language barriers or delayed responses.

Option B — Direct Factory Communication

If you'd like to speak directly with the manufacturer, that's perfectly fine. We don't restrict access. We'll continue supporting the project and ensure proper coordination throughout the process.

Do you provide contracts? What protections do they include?

Yes. Our bilingual contracts in English and Chinese clearly define product specifications and quality standards, pricing and payment terms, delivery schedules and delay responsibilities, remedies for quality issues, confidentiality agreements, and intellectual property ownership.

This ensures both parties share the same understanding from the beginning.

Can I get a refund if I decide to stop the project?

Yes. We offer flexible exit options.

If no actual procurement costs have been incurred during the requirement and planning stage, fees may be partially refundable.

Costs already paid to third parties, such as sampling fees, product testing, or certifications, cannot be refunded.

We'll always explain the nature of each cost and refund policy before the project starts, so there are no surprises later.

How do you handle language and cultural differences?

Our bilingual team understands both Western business expectations and Chinese manufacturing culture.

We help translate technical requirements and commercial terms, prevent misunderstandings caused by cultural differences, explain your needs in a way factories understand, and explain factory feedback in a way you understand.

Simply put, we speak both languages — and both sides of the business. We make communication easier, clearer, and more productive.

Freight & Warehousing

Can I use your freight service without using your sourcing service?

Absolutely. Our freight and warehousing services are completely independent of our sourcing advisory. You can use us purely for shipping, customs clearance, and last-mile delivery — even if you already have your own suppliers in China or elsewhere.

Do you ship from China to New Zealand and Australia?

We have a logistics partner, Nexusfreight, whose ocean freight rates from China to New Zealand and Australia are transparent, with no hidden fees.

Feel free to visit nexusfreight.co.nz.

What freight options do you offer?

Can you collect goods from my supplier in China?

Yes, absolutely. We can arrange pick-up directly from your supplier's factory or warehouse anywhere in China. This is a standard part of our service — we coordinate with your supplier on timing, packaging requirements, and documentation so the goods move smoothly from their dock to our shipping container.

Can you consolidate goods from multiple suppliers?

Absolutely — this is one of our core strengths. We consolidate goods from multiple suppliers into a single shipment: one container, one customs clearance, one delivery, one bill.

We coordinate pickups across China, repack and inspect at our DCs, and ship as one. Lower cost, less admin, no headaches.

What happens when my goods arrive in New Zealand?

We manage the entire arrival process so you don't have to.

Customs & Biosecurity Clearance — If any inspection is required, we manage it on your behalf.

Warehousing — Your goods can be stored at our facility if you're not ready for immediate delivery.

Last-Mile Delivery — We arrange final delivery to your specified address anywhere. We also offer pick & pack services for e-commerce businesses.

Do you offer drop-shipping services?

We don't run your store, but we power your logistics.

We partner with strategically located DCs (Distribution Centres) in Shenzhen and Shanghai. We provide the physical infrastructure to make dropshipping from China work seamlessly.

China Domestic Dispatch: We pick, pack, and ship individual orders directly from our DCs to your Chinese end-customers.

International Dropshipping: We store your inventory in our DCs, and when an order comes in, we ship it internationally directly to your customer's door anywhere in the world.

You stay in control of your sales and customer service; we handle the heavy lifting of storage, picking, packing, and global dispatch.
